AI Assets Scanner 1.7.15

Released May 31, 2026.

Added — Extra Time

  • Opt any URL into a longer probe budget for +1 credit. Step 1 gains a per-URL "Extra Time" checkbox, bulk "Extra Time: all" filters, and a credit badge that counts +1 per Extra-Time-selected URL.
  • Step 4 results gain an "Extra Time" column with a checkbox on every ET-candidate row (selections persist across pages) and an "all ET candidates" toggle.
  • A new "Rescan ET Candidates" button reloads Step 1 pre-loaded with the checked ET URLs — each selected and Extra Time pre-checked — ready for a focused, more-thorough rescan.
  • The Extra Time count is reserved and charged through the SaaS (reserve N+M). When a flagged URL runs out of probe time, the scanning service spawns a fresh-budget continuation job to recover more unloads.

The continuation feature on the scanning service is off by default until enabled.
