AI Assets Scanner 1.7.18

Released June 3, 2026.

Added — redirect-to-canonical URL resolution

  • Bare-domain and redirecting URLs now scan correctly. When a submitted URL server-redirects to its canonical address on the same registrable domain (e.g. a bare domain redirecting to its www host or a language path, like example.com → www.example.com/en), the scanner now follows the redirect and analyses the resolved page instead of the redirect gateway. Previously such targets could return a near-empty result.
  • Transparency on the results table. Each scanned row shows the resolved URL with a small “← resolved from <your submitted URL>” note when the two differ.
  • Safe by design. Cross-domain redirects are never followed — if a URL redirects off its own registrable domain, the submitted URL is scanned as-is. Your reserved and billed domain is unchanged.

Resolution and display only — no rule-output or billing change.
