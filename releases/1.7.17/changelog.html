AI Assets Scanner 1.7.17

Released June 2, 2026.

Fixed & improved — Extra Time UI

  • Step-1 Extra-Time “?” help icon — clicking the help marker no longer toggles the row’s Extra-Time checkbox, and its explanation now appears on hover. (The marker had been nested inside the checkbox label.)
  • The post-scan Extra-Time view now persists — after “Rescan ET Candidates”, leaving the scanner page and returning restores the Extra-Time URL list with your selections, the same way the results screen already persists.
  • Per-URL Credits column — a URL that ran (and was billed for) an Extra-Time continuation now shows its base credit +1, matching the amount charged. Older scans without the new data show base credits only.

UI and per-URL credit display only — no scan-behavior, rule-output, or billing change.
