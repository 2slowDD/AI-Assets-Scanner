AI Assets Scanner 1.7.19

Released June 3, 2026.

Fixed — hotfix for 1.7.18

  • Step-4 results no longer stuck on the “Scanning…” animation. The 1.7.18 release introduced a JavaScript scope error: the per-URL resolved-URL map was declared inside the submit handler but read by the results renderer, throwing a reference error that aborted the transition to the results screen on every scan (clean same-host scans included). The map is now declared at shared scope and the renderer guards the access, so results render normally again.

No change to scan behavior, rules, or billing.
